LEMON PEPPER WINGS!!!!

  • 2 cups oil, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 12 chicken wings

Cooking Instructions

  1. Rinse and clean your chicken/strip off unnecessary bits
  2. Prep your lemon pepper base (use lemon)
  3. Drench wings in the seasoning and prepare them a day before
  4. Cook on 420 degrees for 15 minutes in an air fryer
